Sometimes using the Gender Nuetral Language may seem futile to some people, but believe me, if you repeatedly use the masculine pronoun everywhere, this will only promote male prominence and this will show that women do not come in the relationship you are talking about. At many places,we can see a different kind of change. His / her is used in many places. But is this correct? Because gender equality does not mean equality at some place, but at every place there is to be an equality.

The key rule of thumb here is to avoid gender specific language. There can be lots of alternatives of pronouns like HE and SHE.
